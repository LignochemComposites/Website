About Ecolin AF

Ecolin AF is a sustainable, bio-derived functional filler for superior reinforcement, improved dispersion, and partial Carbon Black replacement in rubber and plastic compounds. Its low density and high compatibility boost mechanical performance in eco-friendly products.

Applications

  • Rubber: Tyres, industrial parts, flooring
  • Plastics: PP, PE, bio-composite blends

Benefits

  • Improved reinforcement & mechanical properties
  • Supports carbon footprint reduction
  • Sustainable & cost-effective
