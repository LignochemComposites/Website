About Ecolin RX

Ecolin RX is a bio-based antioxidant designed to enhance aging resistance and thermal stability in rubber products. It protects rubber compounds from oxidative degradation during processing and service life, reducing long-term wear and maintaining elasticity.

Applications

  • Tyres
  • Rubber seals and gaskets
  • Industrial & automotive rubber components

Benefits

  • Superior antioxidant protection
  • Improves product lifespan
  • Eco-friendly alternative to synthetic antioxidants
